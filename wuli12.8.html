$$\text{(一)①}$$ $$\text{若A、B不发生相对滑动}$$ $$f_{ABmax}=\mu_{1}mg=3N$$ $$f_{A地max}=\mu_{2}(M+m)g=2N$$ $$\dfrac{(f_{AB}-f_{A地})}{M}=\dfrac{F-f_{ab}}{m}$$ $$\because\text{当A、B和地面均不发生滑动时，对于整体，}F=f_{A地}\text{，且}m=M=1kg$$ $$\therefore f_{AB}=\dfrac{1}{2}(F+f_{A地})=F$$ $$\because\text{物体之间均为静摩擦}$$ $$\therefore \begin{cases} F=f_{AB} < f_{ABmax}=3N \\ F=f_{A地} < f_{A地max}=2N \end{cases} $$ $$F<2N$$ $$\text{②}$$ $$2N < F < 4N$$ $$\text{③}$$ $$F > 4N$$ $$\text{④}$$ $$\text{假设可以}$$ $$a_{B}=a_{A}$$ $$\dfrac{f_{AB}}{m} = \dfrac{f_{A地}-f_{AB}}{M}$$ $$\because m=M=1kg$$ $$\therefore \begin{aligned} f_{AB} &= \dfrac{1}{2}f_{A地} \\ &= \dfrac{1}{2}\mu_{2}(m+M)g \\ &= 1N < f_{ABmax} \end{aligned} $$ $$\therefore\text{假设成立}$$ $$\text{(二)①} $$ $$f_{ABmax}=\mu_{1}mg=1N$$ $$f_{A地max}=\mu_{2}(M+m)g=6N$$ $$F-f_{AB} = f_{AB}-f_{A地} = 0$$ $$f_{AB}=\dfrac{1}{2}(F+f_{A地})$$ $$\because\text{A、B均静止}$$ $$\therefore f_{A地}=F$$ $$\therefore \begin{cases} f_{AB} = F < 1N \\ F < f_{A地max} =6N \end{cases} $$ $$\therefore F < 1N$$ $$\text{②}$$ $$\because f_{ABmax}=1N$$ $$\therefore \text{B给A最大1N向右的力}$$ $$\because f_{A地max}=6N$$ $$\therefore \text{A需要6N向右的力才能运动}$$ $$\therefore a_{A}\text{恒为}0$$ $$\therefore \text{不存在}F\text{，使得物体A与物体B一起做共速匀速直线运动.}$$ $$\text{③}$$ $$\text{同上，不存在}F\text{，使得物体A与物体B做不共速直线运动.}$$ $$\text{综上，当}F > 1N\text{时，物体B会向右做匀速直线运动，物体A恒静止.}$$ $$\text{④}$$ $$\text{假设可以}$$ $$\dfrac{f_{AB}}{m}=\dfrac{f_{A地} - f_{AB}}{M}$$ $$f_{AB}=\dfrac{1}{2}f_{A地}=3N > f_{ABmax}$$ $$\therefore\text{不可以}$$
