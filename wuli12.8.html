$$\text{(一)①若A、B不发生相对滑动}$$ $$f_{ABmax}=\mu_{1}mg=3N$$ $$f_{A地max}=\mu_{2}(M+m)g=2N$$ $$\dfrac{(f_{AB}-f_{A地})}{M}=\dfrac{F-f_{ab}}{m}$$ $$\because\text{当A、B和地面均不发生滑动时，对于整体，}F=f_{A地}\text{，且}m=M=1kg$$ $$\therefore f_{AB}=\dfrac{1}{2}(F+f_{A地})=F$$ $$\because\text{物体之间均为静摩擦}$$ $$\therefore \begin{cases} F=f_{AB} < f_{ABmax}=3N \\ F=f_{A地} < f_{A地max}=2N \end{cases} $$ $$F<2N$$ $$\text{②}2N < F < 4N$$ $$\text{③}F > 4N$$ $$\text{④假设可以}$$ $$a_{B}=a_{A}$$ $$\dfrac{f_{AB}}{m} = \dfrac{f_{A地}-f_{AB}}{M}$$ $$\because m=M=1kg$$ $$\therefore \begin{aligned} f_{AB} &= \dfrac{1}{2}f_{A地} \\ &= \dfrac{1}{2}\mu_{2}(m+M)g \\ &= 1N < f_{ABmax} \end{aligned} $$ $$\therefore\text{假设成立}$$
